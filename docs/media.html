Videos

A fun session at TNT Studios with the band "Potassium Man", lead by composer Gabe Evens. This was recorded shortly before the pandemic and I'm so glad we were able complete this project before everything shut down! You can check out all the tracks on the Potassium Man Bandcamp page.

One of the issues with mostly being a sideman is that I don't have many videos of myself; one of the fun perks is seeing videos of me playing with other people. This one, of Phil Fiorio's graduate recital, was during my undergraduate studies at the Eastman School of Music over ten years ago! What a trip to open this time capsule - I was surprised to find a few licks in this solo that I still play to this day (including in my solo on "Always Late")!
